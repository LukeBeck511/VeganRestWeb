Preparation Process

Just like any restaurant, we cut,cook,
and clean. We never have any meat or animal products
in the building so there would be no need for multiple pans for only cooking vegan food.
We also blend smoothies and serve food on safe edible plate leafs for an extra fun touch.
Spices are used regularly and often with multiple different blends available for the customer
so they can add their own taste with the slightly bland base vegan food.

Sustainability

For sustainability we use farm to table methods like locally grown food from smaller farmers/markets.
We also do our best to save on water and recycle and reuse materials. Using the leaf plates we keep
away from using materials for plates and we also grow the leafs and many other smaller plants on sight
and ready to use. We rarely use lights and mostly use open windows and the sun to light bright beautiful restaurants.
